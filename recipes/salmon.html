Salmon Recipe

Description

Salmon are a group of large, soft-finned fish belonging to the Salmonidae family, known for their reddish or pinkish flesh and their ability to migrate from the sea to freshwater to spawn

They are a popular food fish, found in both the Atlantic and Pacific oceans, as well as the rivers and streams connected to them.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 4 (6 ounce) fillets salmon
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 lemon wedges

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Stir garlic powder, basil, and salt together in a small bowl.
  3. Rub the mixture evenly over salmon.
  4. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add salmon and cook until browned and flaky, about 5 minutes per side.
  5. Serve salmon with lemon wedges.
